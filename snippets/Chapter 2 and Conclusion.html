The text, however, resists this kind of control; the lines always seem to scroll faster than the user’s swipes, and if the user tries to scroll the text from a stopped position, they discover that touching a stopped line makes its letters swell and take over the screen for as long as they hold it, making the line unreadable, and making the rest of the PoEMM appear <i>through </i>the specific letter that they initially touched.
